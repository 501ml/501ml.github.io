When you go to a physical bar you have plenty of different bar options to go to.

Now that we can’t go to the bars anymore we need to come up with alternatives.

That’s why we make a virtual bar, a bar accessible from wherever you are.

Our bars are the digital layer on a physical place, you can only access the bar online but they do exist as a real place in the real world.

In the physical world the bar you choose to go to will depend on your personal preferences. To know what bar fits for you, you need to answer a few questions about your personal preferences before entering one of our bars.

The virtuality of our bar gives us the opportunity to adapt the bar’s appearance to everyone’s personal preference.
